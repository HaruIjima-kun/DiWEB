About Archial Law

specialize in residential building design and employ full time qualified staff. We provide services both locally in Tauranga and nationally. A Bay of Plenty owned architectural design company established in 2001, we have designed over 2000 homes and buildings.


Archial Law carries out architectural design work customized to meet your expectations and lifestyle.
